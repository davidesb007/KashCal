Initial F-Droid release.

Privacy-focused calendar with iCloud sync. Your calendar. Your device. Your rules.

Features:
- Privacy-First: Zero analytics, zero tracking
- Offline-First: Works without internet
- iCloud Sync: Native CalDAV support
- Material You: Dynamic theming
- Home Widget: Today's agenda at a glance
- Recurring Events: Full RFC 5545 support
- Search: Full-text search across events
